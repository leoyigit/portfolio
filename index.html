Hello, and welcome to my portfolio! I'm Lèo Yigit Ekiz, a passionate Graphic Designer and Visual Artist born on October 7th, 1988, in the vibrant city of Istanbul, Turkey.

My professional journey began in 2007 when I ventured into the dynamic world of Graphic Design, contributing my creativity to a movie production company in Turkey. As my interest in visual arts intensified, I decided to further my knowledge and skills by enrolling at the prestigious Mimar Sinan Fine Arts Academy in 2007. Here, I delved into the realms of photography and visual arts, broadening my artistic horizons.

Skills

One pivotal chapter of my artistic exploration unfolded in Udine, Italy, where I secured a scholarship as part of the "International Peace Students" project. Collaborating with students from conflicted regions, the project aimed to foster multicultural understanding and peaceful conflict resolution. Immersed in the Italian culture, I studied cinematography, visual communication design, and actively participated in seminars on multiculturalism. This transformative experience not only honed my skills but also elevated my awareness of social and communicational aspects within the realm of art.
